General Insights¶

There have been 22 Fifa WorldCups in total. A tournament has taken place every four year starting from 1930. The only exceptions are 1942 and 1946 which did not have any tournament due to the Second World War.

Most Successful Teams¶

Brazil has had the highest number of world-cup wins (5) in football. Germany and Italy follow close with 4 wins each. Out of all the Fifa WorldCups, 3 teams have won 59% of the tournaments.

Most Popular Venues¶

Rome, Rio de Janeiro and Mexico City are the only Venues that have hosted the Fifa world-cup more than once.

Home Ground Advantage¶

The Host nation has ended up winning the WorldCup tournament 6 out of 22 times. This means that host nations have a 27% chance of winning the World Cup.

Most Goals Scored by a Team¶

Brazil has scored the most World Cup Goals(15). Spain is the only team to have won the World Cup with just having scored 1 goal. Hungary has scored the most goals (4) without winning a World Cup.

import bs4
import requests
import re
import pandas as pd

response = requests.get("https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_FIFA_World_Cup_finals").text

mainlist = []
if response is not None:
    soup = bs4.BeautifulSoup(response,'lxml') #'html.parser')
    for items in soup.find('table', class_='sortable plainrowheaders wikitable').find_all('tr')[1:-4]:
        data = items.find_all(['th','td'])
        try:
            A = '2002' if (data[5].a.text=='Yokohama') else data[0].span.find_next_sibling().text
            #Year.append(data[0].a.text)
            B = data[5].a.text
            C = data[5].a.find_next_sibling().text
            #Hosts.append(data[1].a.text)
            D = data[1].a.text
            E = data[2].a.text
            F = data[3].a.text
        except IndexError:pass
        except AttributeError:pass
        #print(f\"{A}|{B}|{C}|{D}|{E}|{F}\")\n",)
        mainlist.append([A,B,C,D,E,F])
        
df = pd.DataFrame.from_records(mainlist)
df.columns = ['Year','Venue','Host','Winner','Score','Runner-Up']#,'Runner-Up_Score']
df['Winner_Score'] = df.Score.str.split('–').str[0].astype('int32')
df['Runner_Up_Score'] = df.Score.str.split('–').str[1].astype('int32')
df.drop(columns='Score',inplace=True)
df.drop_duplicates('Year',inplace = True)
df.replace('West Germany','Germany',inplace=True)
df.to_csv('FIFA_WorldCup_Results.csv', index=False,encoding='utf_8_sig')
